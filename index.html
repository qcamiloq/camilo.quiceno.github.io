Academic Projects

Development of a training module in column surgery implemented in the Daubara platform

For improve the learning of column surgeries, a new module is designed and developed on the platform Daubara. The simulator is based on medical images and position tracking,as result, neurosurgeons are trained on the insertion of transpedicular screws.

Computational modeling of a tendinous transfer of the posterior tibial muscle, for the correction of fallen foot pathology using the OpenSim software

Analyzing each muscle that work in the flexion of the foot, the tendinous transfer is studied. In the software OpenSim different transfers are executed with the objective of find the best place in the foot bones to apply the best tendon transfer

Biomechanical study of the posture adopted when people use mobile devices. Effects on the cervical region

Using the finite element method (FEM) in ANSYS, the common postures that people adopt when they use the smartphone are studied with the objective of observe the effects on the cervical region
